Emergency Protocol

DOs

  • Stay Calm: Keep the patient still to slow venom spread.
  • Remove Constrictions: Take off rings, watches, or tight clothing near the bite.
  • Keep Immobilized: Keep the bitten limb at or slightly below heart level.
  • Seek Medical Help: Get to a hospital immediately; antivenom is the only effective treatment.
  • Note Details: Remember snake's appearance or take a photo from a safe distance.

DON'Ts

  • Do NOT Cut: Never cut the bite wound or attempt to suck out the venom.
  • Do NOT Tourniquet: Avoid applying tight tourniquets which can cause tissue damage.
  • No Ice/Heat: Do not apply ice, cold packs, or heat to the bite area.
  • No Alcohol/Caffeine: Do not give the victim anything to eat or drink except water.
  • Do NOT Chase: Do not try to catch or kill the snake.
